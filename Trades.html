Long Term Investments
I invest in an S&P 500 tracker mutual fund (unhedged) and so I created a chart using MATLAB to monitor its performance. Each plotted point represents a purchase made, and the vertical lines show a new year, with the percentage growth in the previous year. I try to leverage profits using technical analysis to time investments, which this chart can give an insight into. I am also looking to leverage with currency hedging when I feel that the USDGBP is high in relation to average historic levels.

Here is a hedged fund that uses currency forwards, options and currency swaps, to track the S&P 500 index. The graph was creating in MATLAB using data from the Financial Times, tracking the fund price in GBP and the actual index price in USD. The index price is adjusted to be equivalent to the Fund price on day 0, and subsequent prices are divided by this constant to allow for a simple comparison.
